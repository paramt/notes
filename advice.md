running list of advice I've received / principles I try to follow 

# life advice
- speak broadly about what you like, and narrowly about what you don't like 
	- taken to its limit, avoid speaking about what you dislike 
- think about how to think -- metacognition
	- work on / evolve your brain's thoughts 
- build in public
- "be cringe"
	- don't let  your own ego get in your way 
	- the haters won't stop hating on you no matter how successful you get
- reach out to people
	- people are willing to talk to you and help you out more than you assume 

# career advice 
- keep track of everything you do
	- for resume points, but also to be able to share with manager weekly 
- make your manager look good
- communicate frequently with updates, that's the only way to show that progress has been made
